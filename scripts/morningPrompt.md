# Morning Xome Learning Brief Prompt

Use this prompt for a Codex chat follow-up or scheduled automation:

Generate 5-10 simple learning items for a new Xome QA engineering intern who is new to real estate.

Focus on:
- What Xome does and how its product helps users.
- Real-estate terms explained in plain English.
- Product workflows that may need better testing.
- Engineering, data, AI, search, automation, integration, or reliability updates.
- Questions a startup founder or user would ask.
- Talking points for a product manager, engineer, or business stakeholder.

Keep the language simple. For each item include:
- Simple summary.
- Why it matters.
- Technical angle.
- QA idea.
- Founder/user question.
- Stakeholder talking point.
- Source link.
